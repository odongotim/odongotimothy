Engagements

Lira University Pageantry 3rd Edition

He participated in the Lira University Pageantry 3rd Edition, held under the theme “Youth Voices Against Substance Abuse.” Through his commitment to advocacy, leadership, and positive influence, he emerged as Mr. Popularity Lira University. In this role, he continues to champion youth empowerment initiatives aimed at combating substance abuse within Lira University and across Uganda. He works with peers, student groups, and community stakeholders to raise awareness, promote responsible choices, and create supportive environments where young people can thrive free from the dangers of drug and substance misuse. His ongoing efforts reflect his passion for community impact, youth leadership, and building a healthier, more informed generation.

Economic Empowerment Lecture – Ebenezer University

He attended a high-level economic lecture at Ebenezer University, an event that brought together more than 600 students from various institutions across Lira. The session was led by Dr. Okello Sharon Ngenjwa (GirlfromOyam) and Otolo Sedrick, both recognized for their work in youth economic transformation. During the lecture, he was introduced to Musevenomics, an economic framework focused on fighting poverty, strengthening financial resilience, and promoting sustainable economic empowerment among young people. The insights gained from this session enhanced his understanding of practical economic strategies that support youth development, innovation, and long-term prosperity. He applies these learnings in his ongoing engagements and initiatives aimed at empowering fellow youth and contributing to community growth.

Youth Advocacy Radio Talkshow – Unity FM

He participated in a youth-focused radio talkshow on Unity FM, aimed at educating the youth of Northern Uganda about the dangers of drugs and toxic substances. The show, hosted on the Youth Hour by Dorcus Akullu Ecel, provided a platform to share practical advice on making positive life choices and promoting healthy lifestyles. Through this engagement, he contributed to raising awareness, inspiring resilience, and encouraging peers to stay focused on personal growth and community development.
